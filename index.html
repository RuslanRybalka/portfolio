Rubic's cube timer

I like to solve Rubic's cube, so I decided to create a timer to track the time I spend solving the puzzle. It includes such fields as "best time", "current time", "average time of last 10 tries" and a list of last 10 tries. Also You can delete last try from list to ignore it in calculating average result.
